Mayon Volcano

A popular tourist spot, it is renowned for its "perfect cone" because of its symmetric conical shape, and is regarded as very sacred in Philippine mythology. Book now!

Banaue Rice Terraces

The Banaue Rice Terraces are located on the mountains of Ifugao and were carved there about 2,000 years ago by the ancestors of the present day Philippines. Book now!

Chocolate Hills

The hills are located throughout the towns of Carmen, Batuan and Sagbayan and consist of about 1,776 mounds of the same general shape. Book now!

Taal Volcano

Its unexplained shape and location on an island within a lake within an island, makes it a unique geologic wonder, enthralling thousands of tourists and geologists yearly. Book now!

Boracay

Boracay Island, one of the top beaches in the Philippines and one of the best Visayas tourist spots, is blessed with a long stretch of powdery white sand beach, crystal clear and azure waters, a stunning sunset, and more. Book now!

El Nido, Palawan

El Nido is known for its stunning lagoons, white sand beaches, rocky islets, towering limestone cliffs, and glasslike waters teeming with marine life. Book now!
